Our Story

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 1996, Golden Fork has been a beacon of fine dining excellence for over three decades. Our passion for quality ingredients and timeless recipes has made us a beloved destination for food enthusiasts from around the world.

Every dish tells a story of tradition, crafted with the finest locally-sourced ingredients and prepared by our world-class chefs who bring decades of experience to your table.
